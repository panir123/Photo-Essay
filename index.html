Living together, alone

By Zunairah

A single metal chair sits in the backyard following a recent snowfall.
No one has bothered to pick it up yet.

The indoor thermometer displays the current temperature, at 10:00 am in the morning.

My aunt prepares a meal in the kitchen, viewed through a small interior
window from the adjacent room.

The right side of the table. A laptop, a single mug, and a pair of headphones
are neatly arranged on the right side of the table.

The left side of the same table is covered in a scattered collection of drinks,
mugs, papers, and open notebooks.

My sister sits at the upright piano in the living room practicing a musical piece by herself.

Two identical electric toothbrushes sit in the same holder; one brush head shows
significant wear while the other remains relatively new.

Several pairs of winter boots, sneakers, shoes belonging to different family
members are piled together by the front door.

The "Happy Place" sign and the temperature display are visible on the wall during the night hours while the rest of the house is quiet.

The same backyard chair is visible at night, no one has moved at an inch.
